Marcos de Barros

Lawyer, project manager on child justice at Terre des hommes Europe

I am a laywer currently working as a Project Officer at Terre des hommes Regional Hub, in Budapest, Hungary. After graduating in Law in Brazil, I continued my studies in France, Italy, and Hungary while pursuing a master's degree in theory and practice of human rights at UCLY (Université Catholique de Lyon). Between January 2020 and November 2021, I coordinated i-RESTORE, a regional project focused on the use of restorative justice with child victims in Albania, Greece, and Romania. I am now the regional coordinator of CLEAR-Rights, a project focused on enhancing the quality of legal assistance for children in Europe.
